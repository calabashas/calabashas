tomato basil salad

fresh, tangy


Ingredients:

4 cups cherry tomatoes, halved

1/2 wedge miyokos or another plant-based mozzarella

1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped

2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

Salt and pepper to taste

Croutons (optional)


Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.

3. Pour the dressing over the tomato mixture and toss gently to coat.

4. Let the salad sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

5. Toss in croutons. Enjoy!


Tips

- basalmic vinegar:  Cucina & Amore Balsamic Vinegar of Modena

- olive oil:  Atlas Organic Cold Pressed Moroccan Extra Virgin Olive Oil
